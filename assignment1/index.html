“What is your impression of the intended primary goal of the interactive experience?’’

My impression is that the intended audience for this website is design historians, researchers, architects, students studying architecture, and architectural enthusiasts, particularly in the range of teenagers to adults in their mid-30s. This assumption is based on how imaginative and artistic the web design is; it aims to draw in young users to visit the interactive websites and learn about the building. As a result, the website contains a variety of mediums (video, music, 3D, and effects), preventing it from becoming monotonous. Their user experience is designed for website-savvy individuals.

“How does the interactive experience communicate this primary goal?”

I believe the primary goal is to teach users about the historical building, not just how it was created but also about its context; why was the building built? Why is it known as the 'Walt Disney Concert Hall'? Why was it built in a certain way? What kind of music do they play in the concert hall? Etc. The designers successfully communicated these goals without having to design in a traditional form, instead using interactive media such as 3D models, films, and music.

  • 3D:The use of 3D models allows designers to portray the building from a variety of angles and perspectives, which is very useful for international visitors or those who have never seen it. By zooming in and out, viewers may have a more immersive and detailed perspective of the building, giving them a better sense of how it looks. This successfully communicated the primary purpose by improving their users' visualisations, demonstrating a more detailed and immersive representation of the subject than typical 2D images.
  • Video:The use of video showcased a taped interview with architect Frank Gehry. He was asked questions about the music hall's development process and explained why he chose certain features for the hall. Not only is this an enjoyable way to learn about the building, but it also allows viewers to connect with him. Understand the deeper meaning of why he designed the structure in that way. It appears that they saw this building as art, which is why the filmed video revealed the deeper meaning of why they built it that way.
  • Sound:When visitors enter the webpage, they will be greeted by music. The classical music that replays and changes as you scroll down to the next chapters reveals what kind of music was played in the Walt Disney Concert Hall. This medium may enhance the interactive experience by allowing users to be 'in the mood' or experience nostalgia, as classical music frequently conveys a feeling of history. With this medium included in the website, the designers have successfully communicated their primary objectives.
